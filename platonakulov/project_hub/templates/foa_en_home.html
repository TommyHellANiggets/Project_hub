“Fears Of Alone” is a collection of psychological horror games inspired by real stories, with the addition of characters' insane dreams and phobias at a certain period of their lives.

Our project is the result of inspiration from the "Fears To Fathom" game series by the creator "Rayll." We strongly recommend purchasing and playing games from this creator.

Feel the atmosphere of horror games that won't let you breathe calmly. Download the free demo version of any part of "Fears Of Alone" right now and immerse yourself in the nightmares experienced by the main characters.

Lurking In The Darkness

Two students went on a summer camping trip. It was a cozy place where kind people rested. However, over time, they began to notice something strange. You have to uncover the secrets of this forest and find out what happened that night. A frightening yet cozy adventure awaits you, where every rustle could be your last.

In the city where shadows come alive and whispers of the past echo through the deserted streets, you find yourself trapped. Your only way out is to uncover the mysteries hidden in the city's heart. The shadows watch your every move. Will you escape the City of Shadows?
